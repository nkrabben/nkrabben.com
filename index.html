About

I am a digital cultural heritage curator who helps libraries, archives, and museums adapt to digital collection. When average people manage tens of thousands of images, emails, documents, videos, and other digital objects that will form the basis of future collections, cultural organizations must learn to manage these new materials with new tools at an unprecedented scale. Through my work, I help to address these challenges by building the necessary technology, policy, and culture.
